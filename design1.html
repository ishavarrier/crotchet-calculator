Crotchet Calculator

My sister and I both love to crotchet, and have begun to make projects for our freinds and family. However, it has been difficult to accurately make our tops the right size as we cannot measure easily. So, I created this application with Javascript, HTML, and CSS to help us and any others easily know the perfect amount of stitches to guarantee the right fit.
